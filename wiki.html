Deiform
The Deiform Network is a community that values uniqueness and creativity in public figures and projects. Our aim is to identify and invest in promising ventures early on, and provide support for their growth and development with high-quality content. To join our community, we require that you have a distinct public image and produce games or projects that showcase originality and unsettling or off-putting themes. To maintain your status within the Deiform Network, you must craft and consistently maintain a unique persona that represents you in public. We place great emphasis on consistency, ensuring that you remain true to your image and persona at all times. We also expect members to steer clear of drama and refrain from creating any within our community. Your rank within the network depends on the level of effort you put into your projects. This includes consistent levels of building, cohesiveness in storytelling and themes, and active participation in the community. Additionally, we highly value creativity and encourage our members to bring their unique style, image, and story to the table. By joining the Deiform Network, you will be part of a community that fosters and supports unique and creative individuals and projects. We believe that our members can help each other grow and achieve their goals, and we look forward to seeing what you have to offer.
